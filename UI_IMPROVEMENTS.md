## ğŸ¯ Animation Principles Used

### Timing Functions:
- **cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)**: Natural, smooth acceleration/deceleration
- **ease-out**: Quick start, slow end (good for entrances)
- **ease-in-out**: Symmetrical acceleration (good for loops)

### Duration Standards:
- **0.3s**: Quick interactions (hover, focus)
- **0.5s-0.8s**: Medium animations (page elements, cards)
- **2s-20s**: Slow ambient effects (glows, shimmers)

### Animation Delays:
- **Staggered by 0.1s-0.2s**: Creates flowing sequence
- **Max 0.8s delay**: Keeps user engaged
