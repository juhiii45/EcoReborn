Transforming discarded textiles into sustainable fabrics through circular innovation.

Ecoreborn collects discarded textiles, converts them into usable fibers, and re-spins them into new fabric — reducing waste and conserving water and energy.

Why Choose Circular Textiles?

Reduces Waste

Diverts thousands of tons of textiles from landfills, transforming waste into valuable resources.

Conserves Water

Uses up to 95% less water compared to virgin textile production, protecting precious water resources.

Saves Energy

Dramatically reduces energy consumption by eliminating resource extraction and processing.

Minimizes Virgin Resources

Reduces dependence on new cotton, polyester, and other raw materials, preserving ecosystems.

Our Circular Process

From discarded clothes to premium re-spun fabric in four sustainable steps.

1

Collection

We collect discarded textiles through community drop-offs, partnerships with brands, and organized collection drives at schools and organizations.

2

Sorting & Cleaning

Textiles are carefully sorted by fiber type and color, then thoroughly cleaned using eco-friendly processes to prepare them for recycling.

3

Mechanical/Chemical Recycling

Using advanced mechanical shredding or chemical dissolution, we break down fabrics into high-quality fibers ready for new life.

4

Re-spinning & Finishing

Recycled fibers are spun into premium yarns and woven or knitted into beautiful, durable fabrics ready for fashion and textile applications.
