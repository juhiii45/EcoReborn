Frequently Asked Questions

What types of textiles do you accept?

We accept all types of fabric including cotton, polyester, wool, and blended materials. Items can include clothing, bed linens, curtains, and other textile products.

How much does fabric recycling cost?

For individuals and small organizations, collection is often free. For businesses, pricing depends on volume and location. Contact us for a custom quote.

What is the minimum order for custom fabric?

Our minimum order for custom re-spun fabric is 100 meters. We can accommodate larger orders and provide volume discounts.

How long does the recycling process take?

From collection to finished fabric, the process typically takes 4-8 weeks depending on volume and specifications.

Can you help our school organize a collection drive?

Yes! We provide free support for educational institutions including collection bins, educational materials, and pickup services. Contact us to get started.

Is recycled fabric quality comparable to new fabric?

Absolutely. Our re-spun fabrics meet or exceed industry standards for durability, appearance, and performance while being more sustainable.
